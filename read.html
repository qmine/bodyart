From the artist

As I paint, I am trying to conjure up a way of accessing that space. So the canvas becomes a portal or gateway that draws the viewer through the canvas into a transcendent dimension. Essentially, each piece is an attempt to capture an emotion. It is connected to an idea of ‘soul’ and an attempt to visualise those feelings such as love and loss that make us human.
